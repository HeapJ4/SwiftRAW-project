SwiftRAW is an offline Android gallery for reviewing camera RAW files directly from an SD card or USB reader.

Choose a folder through Android's system picker, browse cached thumbnails, open embedded JPEG previews with pinch-to-zoom, and export previews without changing the original files.

Supported formats include Nikon NEF/NRW, Canon CR2/CR3, Sony ARW/SR2/SRF, Fujifilm RAF, Olympus ORF, Panasonic RW2, Pentax PEF, DNG and other common RAW extensions. Standard image formats are also supported.

SwiftRAW does not request network access, include analytics or upload photographs. All processing happens locally on the device.
